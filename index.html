Who We Are

Empowering Africa's Brightest Minds Through Fully Funded Education

We don't just find scholarships. We create opportunities. The African Global Scholarship Foundation exists to identify exceptionally talented African students, assess their potential, secure admission into world-class universities, and sponsor their educational journey from admission to graduation.

We believe that financial limitations should never prevent brilliant minds from accessing world-class education. Our mission is simple: find Africa's brightest minds, invest in them, and develop future leaders.

Our Vision

To build the largest network of African scholars educated at the world's leading universities and equipped to transform Africa.

Our Mission

To discover, assess, mentor, sponsor, and develop outstanding African students by providing full educational sponsorship at top universities in the United States.

Why We Exist

Africa is home to millions of talented young people with extraordinary potential. They lack neither intelligence nor ambition — only access. We exist to bridge that gap.

What We Do

Six commitments, one mission

Our foundation is committed to ensuring that gifted African students receive the education, mentorship, and support necessary to become leaders in government, business, technology, healthcare, education, and entrepreneurship.

Talent Identification

Nationwide and continent-wide searches to identify exceptional students with leadership potential and academic excellence.

Assessment & Selection

Every candidate undergoes academic assessment, leadership evaluation, character assessment, vision review, and personal interviews.

University Placement

Our team works directly with leading universities to secure admission opportunities for selected scholars.

Full Educational Sponsorship

Tuition, accommodation, books, travel, health insurance, living stipends, and academic support services — fully covered.

Leadership Development

Continuous leadership training designed to prepare scholars for positions of influence and impact.

Alumni Network

Graduates join a lifelong network committed to advancing Africa's development through collaboration and service.

Where We Work

A continental search for extraordinary talent

We run identification programs across Africa's regions — West, East, Southern, Central and North — partnering with schools, communities and local organisations to find students whose talent might otherwise go unseen. Every scholar we sponsor carries their community's story into rooms it has never been in before.

Our Scholarship Model

From first application to lifelong alumni network, every scholar moves through the same disciplined seven-step journey.

01

Apply for Assessment

Every journey begins with a simple application and entrance assessment.

02

Academic & Leadership Evaluation

Candidates are evaluated on academic strength, character and leadership potential.

03

Selection into the Scholars Program

Only the most outstanding candidates are selected to move forward.

04

University Admission Placement

We work with our university partners to secure admission for each scholar.

05

Full Sponsorship Award

Tuition, housing, and living costs are covered from admission to graduation.

06

Leadership Development Program

Scholars receive ongoing mentorship and leadership training throughout their studies.

07

Graduation & Alumni Network

Graduates join a lifelong community advancing Africa's development together.

Message from the Founder

"I believe Africa's greatest resource is not its oil, minerals, or land. Its greatest resource is its people."

Across our continent are young men and women with extraordinary potential, waiting for an opportunity to shine. This foundation was established to ensure that talent is never limited by circumstance, and that exceptional African students can access world-class education regardless of their financial background.

Our commitment is not simply to sponsor students. Our commitment is to raise a generation of leaders who will shape the future of Africa and the world.

Dr. Mozac Abraham
