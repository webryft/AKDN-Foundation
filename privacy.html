Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

On this page

1. Overview

The African Global Scholarship Foundation ("AGSF", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of every applicant, scholar, donor, and visitor who interacts with our website. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

2. Information We Collect

When you apply for assessment, ask a question, or otherwise interact with our website, we may collect:

  • Your full name, age, email address, and phone number, submitted through our application form.
  • Your entrance assessment responses and results.
  • Any questions or messages you submit through our "Ask a Question" form.
  • Basic technical information such as browser type, used to keep the site functioning correctly.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Process your scholarship application and generate your unique exam number.
  • Administer and grade your entrance assessment.
  • Communicate with you regarding your application status and results, including sending your full results report by email.
  • Respond to questions submitted through our website.
  • Improve our programs and outreach to better serve future applicants.

4. Data Storage & Security

All applicant and assessment data is stored securely using Google Firebase, a cloud platform with industry-standard security practices. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. Applicant records are indexed primarily by email address to allow our administrative team to locate and respond to your application efficiently.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information may only be shared with:

  • AGSF staff and authorized administrators directly involved in evaluating applications.
  • University or partner institutions, but only if you are selected to advance in the scholarship process and only with your knowledge.
  • Service providers (such as our cloud hosting provider) who help us operate the website, under appropriate confidentiality obligations.

6. Entrance Assessment Data

Your entrance assessment responses, score, and exam number are stored in connection with your application and are visible only to authorized AGSF administrators. Please keep your exam number confidential, as it can be used to retrieve your results. A full results report will be sent to the email address you provided during registration.

7. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information held by AGSF, subject to any legal or administrative requirements that may apply to scholarship application records.

8. Cookies

Our website does not currently use tracking or advertising cookies. Any technical data stored locally on your device, such as session information used to keep you logged into an ongoing exam session, is used solely to support core site functionality.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our data practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please reach out to us through the "Ask a Question" form available on our homepage.
